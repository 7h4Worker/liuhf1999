## Publication Details

**Authors:** H. Liu, Z. Wang, R. Li, et al.

**Venue:** IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBC), 2024

**DOI:** [10.1109/EMBC53108.2024.10781783](https://doi.org/10.1109/EMBC53108.2024.10781783)

## Abstract

We encode binocular cues directly into dual-view VR stimuli, reducing the number of driving frequencies while increasing target separability. The resulting paradigm improves recognition accuracy under motion and depth changes, paving the way for practical immersive SSVEP control schemes.

## Key Contributions

- **Dual-view SSVEP modulation** that encodes nine VR targets with just two frequencies
- **Robust SSVEP control** in immersive environments
- **Enhanced target separability** through binocular encoding
- **Improved recognition accuracy** under motion and depth variations
