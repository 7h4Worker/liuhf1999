SSVEP-based brain-controlled systems for the 2025 Brain-Computer Interface Conference (BCIA, Shanghai), achieving Third Prize in both Brain-Controlled Car and Robotic Arm categories.

**Highlights:**
- Unity-based integrated platform with real-time SSVEP decoding
- Low-cost hardware setup
- Digital twin for car simulation and control
