This project demonstrates real-time brain-controlled systems developed for the 2025 China BCI Competition, achieving bronze medals in both vehicle and robotic arm categories.

## Brain-Controlled Vehicle

![Unity scene configuration]({{ '/images/小车_unity场景配置.jpg' | relative_url }})

- **Interface:** Unity-based SSVEP stimulus presentation with real-time visual feedback
- **Control:** 8-target SSVEP paradigm for directional commands
- **Algorithm:** FBCCA with ONNX-deployed neural network for online classification

## Brain-Controlled Robotic Arm

![Competition team photo]({{ '/images/机械臂赛场合影1.jpg' | relative_url }})

- **Task:** Multi-DOF robotic arm manipulation using brain signals
- **Achievement:** Bronze medal at 2025 BCIA Shanghai Competition
- **Team:** Led the ShanghaiTech University team as technical lead
